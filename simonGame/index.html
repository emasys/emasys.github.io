How to play

  • Toggle the switch button to begin a new game.
  • AI will select a random color which will light up and play a sound.
  • Computer stores sequence of selected colors, you are expected to remember it.
  • Click colors corresponding to computer's moves in the right order.
  • If you click a wrong color, game restarts.
  • Otherwise, computer replays previous moves, adding a new move.
  • You will repeat steps 4-6.
  • If you reach 20 successful moves, you win.
  • GOOD LUCK!
